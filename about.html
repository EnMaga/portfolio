About

Researcher at the intersection of ecology, remote sensing, and data science.

I specialise in Earth observation-driven monitoring of regenerative agriculture, combining satellite and drone data to understand landscape change and ecological function.

My work spans machine learning, geospatial analysis, UAV survey design, and the development of workflows that turn complex data into evidence for biodiversity and sustainable land management.

Desktop GUI pipelines for Sentinel-1 (SAR) and Sentinel-2 (optical) — SNAP ARD processing, cloud masking, biophysical variables and spectral indices as Cloud-Optimised GeoTIFFs. This work was accepted for a poster and annals article at ASITA 2026 in the session “New sensors, platforms and products in remote sensing.”

My background in natural sciences and ecology helps me connect technical analysis with environmental interpretation and practical applications.
