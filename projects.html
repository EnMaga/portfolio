Projects

Selected work at the edge of ecology, sensing, and geospatial intelligence.

Regenerative agriculture monitoring

Satellite–UAV fusion for practice verification

Developing workflows that combine Sentinel-1, multispectral, thermal, and UAV LiDAR data to verify regenerative agricultural practices and assess biodiversity indicators.

WebGIS & geospatial systems

GeoServer and MapStore-based platforms

Building and maintaining geospatial platforms for research and applied land management, with an emphasis on visualisation, indexing, and analysis pipelines.

Dryland ecology

Vegetation pattern formation

Study of the formation of regular vegetation patterns in drylands, including drought experiments with Paspalum vaginatum, remote sensing analysis (RGB, multispectral, thermal), mathematical modelling, and presentation at NWO Life 2023.

MSc research

Drought effects on plant communities

Investigating how drought and atmospheric nutrient deposition together alter plant traits, diversity, soil microbiomes, community structure, and ecosystem functioning in grassland systems.

MSc fieldwork

Bird abundance surveys in forest habitats

Comparing traditional bird survey methods with acoustic recording in a meta-analysis to evaluate ecological monitoring effectiveness.

Bachelor’s thesis

Nardus grassland germination research

Preliminary germination and post-germination studies of species-rich Natura 2000 Nardus grassland species for restoration in the Trentino region.

Remote sensing workflows

Scalable analysis pipelines

Creating reproducible analysis chains in Python and Earth Engine for large-scale geospatial monitoring across agricultural and ecological systems.
